About Jacque Fresco

Jacque Fresco (1916-2017) was an American futurist and visionary thinker. He dedicated his life to designing a comprehensive plan for social change, focusing on sustainable living and a resource-based economy. Fresco's innovative designs aim to ensure a high standard of living for all while preserving natural resources.

Fresco's Ideas and Impact

Jacque Fresco's work has inspired global movements and organizations dedicated to creating a sustainable future. His vision of a resource-based economy emphasizes the efficient use of resources, advanced technology, and a high standard of living for all. His ideas continue to influence urban planning, environmental sustainability, and social systems worldwide.
